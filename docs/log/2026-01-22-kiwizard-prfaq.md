## Appendix: Fortune Telling Reference

A reference guide to popular fortune telling types that could inspire Kiwizard agents.

---

### 1. Horoscope / Astrology

**What it is:**
Uses the 12 zodiac signs (Ariesâ€“Pisces) based on birth date to give daily/weekly/monthly forecasts and love/career/money horoscopes.

**Short description:**
The most common Western-style fortune-telling, ranging from short "today's horoscope" blurbs to long monthly forecasts written by astrologers.

---

### 2. Natal Chart & Transit Reading

**What it is:**
Uses your exact birth date, time, and place to calculate a natal (birth) chart, then interprets personality, life themes, and current trends based on planetary transits.

**Short description:**
A much more personalized form of astrology than simple sun-sign horoscopes, often used as a deep dive into personality plus timing for love, career, and life events.

---

### 3. Tarot Reading

**What it is:**
Draws cards from a tarot deck and interprets their symbolic meaning to reflect your current situation, mindset, near future, and possible advice.

**Short description:**
Commonly used to gain insight or guidance about "what's going on right now" rather than fixed predictions; online versions offer both auto-draws and live readings with human readers.

---

### 4. Psychic / Fortune-Teller Reading

**What it is:**
One-on-one sessions (chat/phone/video) with psychics, astrologers, tarot readers, or spiritual advisors to ask about love, work, money, or the future.

**Short description:**
Similar to an online "fortune-teller shop": platforms aggregate many readers, you browse profiles and reviews, then pay per minute or per session for personalized readings.

---

### 5. Numerology

**What it is:**
Uses your name and birth date to calculate numbers like your Life Path Number or Destiny Number, then interprets these as life themes and tendencies.

**Short description:**
A number-based fortune system analogous to Western-style saju, focusing on life purpose, personality, and timing (e.g., personal year numbers) using simple calculations.

---

### 6. Compatibility / Love Match

**What it is:**
Compares two people's zodiac signs, birth charts, or sometimes just names/birth dates to assess romantic or relationship compatibility.

**Short description:**
Often used for "How good is our match?" questions; ranges from simple zodiac compatibility scores to full synastry-style relationship analysis.
