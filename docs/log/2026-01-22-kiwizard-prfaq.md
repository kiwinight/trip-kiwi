### Q: What could kill this project?

**A:**

1. **AI quality doesn't meet expectations**
   - If MBTI results feel random, or fortune telling feels generic, users won't trust it
   - Mitigation: Heavy investment in prompt engineering, start narrow (2 activities), iterate based on feedback

2. **No social traction**
   - If users don't share to Feed, the social layer dies
   - Mitigation: Make Artifacts beautiful and shareable, add incentives for sharing, consider private-by-default option

3. **OpenAI/Google ships this feature**
   - If ChatGPT adds a dedicated "personality tests" mode with shareable results
   - Mitigation: Move fast, build brand, focus on community/social features they won't build

4. **User fatigue with AI**
   - If AI hype dies down and people stop caring
   - Mitigation: Focus on entertainment value, not "AI" as the selling point
